Datamosh Selfie

Interactive Video, size variable

A method of self-reflection using software.
A sustained, interactive glitch.

The visitor will encounter a very obvious webcam that is connected to software that breaks and melts the pixels in real-time. One can see their own likeness become a moving painting, and play with the broken webcam feed in real time.
Skintone, inherited facial features, size, hair texture, and so on are all put into question by the movements performed intentionally or accidentally in front of the webcam.
Everything you do becomes a live painting, a commentary on identity politics.
The only way to maintain the semblance of a cohesive visual identity is to be in front of the camera standing very still, compelling inaction.
NFT portraits of PNG, GIF, or MP4 file types may be requested.
