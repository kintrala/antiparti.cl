Harmony Vibes

Social Installation, 8m x 5m x 3m

 

 I am interested in the sonification of any object, the song that paintings can evoke in the mind,
and the combination of these particular sensory experiences for the purpose of manufacturing a reproducible situation addressing these perceptual events in unison. 

For this project I want to build a sound device that is made up of found conductive objects and living plant materials
that visitors can touch.

The device will be presented in the form of a gently lit sitting canopy garden that is touch-sensitive, with hidden speakers,
where multiple hands can play together. The goal is to create a gentle environment that is enjoyable and accessible to people of all ages and hearing abilities and their loved ones.

Rather than directly connecting the natural sound of the object to a microphone, the goal is to use filters to gently modulate the output. 

Without given instructions for the device, visitors will find softness in their own interventions, produced by their own curiosity,
and so practically demonstrating what is required from people to save the world from extinction.
