is W A Y reflections

Series of Watercolor on Paper of varying sizes

 

By dreaming about fluidity, we can think about the instant beauty of light reflecting on water. 
It is so simple, and still enjoyable to our eyes even after millennia of human Art.

I would like to spend some time painting my impressions of the interaction between members of our team
inside the architectural masterpiece that is the ACAC, with the water present within the architecture as the medium
for my colours on locally sourced washi.

Several years ago, while bookworming at the library of the Rachofsky Collection in Dallas
I came across the treasure of books signed by Tadao Ando, and recognized the transcendental qualities
of his architecture that, until that moment, was only a dream to experience.  

After reading his words, and crying a lot, Ando sensei’s philosophy inspired me
to look even deeper into is W A Y shape, and to see a similar intention with my artistic practice. 

By then I had already been drawing the W A Y shape for almost a decade,
and even today I still see no end to the language of harmony that it can express.
